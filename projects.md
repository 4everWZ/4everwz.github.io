<p class="project-meta">January 2024 – July 2024 · Beijing Elite Intelligence Company & North China Institute of Science and Technology</p>

## Baiyangdian Ecological Internet of Things Three-dimensional Monitoring System

<p class="project-meta">June 2023 – January 2024 · Hebei Province Key Scientific Research and Development Project (Project Number: 19270318D)</p>

Worked on object detection systems for mining trucks and low-altitude drone monitoring under practical efficiency constraints.

- Developed object detection for mining trucks using Yolov7tiny (Darknet), improving recognition accuracy by 2% with minimal FPS loss.
- Enhanced visible-infrared object detection with YOLOv8 for drone-based low-altitude monitoring, achieving 65% mAP at 30 FPS on a laptop.
